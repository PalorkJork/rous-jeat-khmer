Helpful Information

How Can We Help You?

Find answers to common questions about our food, delivery service, reservations and events.

1. What type of food do you serve?

We serve traditional Khmer dishes such as Fish Amok, Beef Lok Lak, Khmer Rice Noodles, Samlor Korko, grilled food, desserts and local drinks.

2. Can I order food online?

Yes. Visit the Order Online page, select your preferred dishes and enter your contact details.

3. Do you offer delivery?

Yes. Delivery is available in Phnom Penh and nearby areas. Delivery fees start from $1.50.

4. How long does delivery take?

Delivery normally takes approximately 30 to 60 minutes depending on your location and order size.

5. Can I reserve a table online?

Yes. Use the Table Reservation page to select your preferred date, time and number of guests.

6. Do you provide catering services?

Yes. We offer catering packages for meetings, birthday parties, weddings and family events.

7. What payment methods are available?

Customers can pay by cash or bank transfer. Additional payment options can be added later.

8. Can I request a private dining area?

Yes. Select the private-area option when submitting your reservation or event request.

9. What are your opening hours?

We are open from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM from Monday to Friday and from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekends.

10. How can I contact the restaurant?

Visit the Contact page, call +855 12 345 678 or email info@khmertaste.com.
